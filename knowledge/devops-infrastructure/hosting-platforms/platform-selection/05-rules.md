# Rules: Platform Selection

## PS-001: Match Platform to Team Skills
**Condition:** Selecting hosting platform
**Action:** Choose platform that matches team's operational capabilities
**Rationale:** A platform your team can't operate effectively will cause more issues than it solves
**Consequences:** Violation causes operational incidents from mismatched expertise

## PS-002: Consider Total Cost
**Condition:** Evaluating hosting options
**Action:** Calculate total cost including infrastructure, managed services, and operational time
**Rationale:** Free-tier or cheap platforms may require more operational time than paid alternatives
**Consequences:** Violation underestimates true cost of hosting

## PS-003: Plan for Migration
**Condition:** Choosing hosting platform
**Action:** Evaluate migration difficulty before committing
**Rationale:** Platform lock-in makes future hosting changes expensive
**Consequences:** Violation traps application in unsuitable platform
