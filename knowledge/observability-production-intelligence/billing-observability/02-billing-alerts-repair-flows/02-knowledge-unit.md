# Executive Summary

Production billing systems need alerts for webhook failures, subscription drift, queue backlogs, and failed billing jobs. Support teams need repair flows: manual webhook replay, subscription sync, and entitlement repair. Architecture proposals should include "how we know it broke" and "how support can repair it."
